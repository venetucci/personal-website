Michelle Venetucci is an anthropologist studying the technology industry in the San Francisco Bay Area. Her research interests include technology company workflows and workplace culture, and the social contexts of how new technologies are developed in and around Silicon Valley.

She's currently a PhD candidate in the Yale Anthropology department, with an emphasis in Science & Technology Studies. She was previously a product designer at GitHub, Lyft, and thoughtbot. She serves on the board of Noisebridge Hackerspace in San Francisco and sometimes she does fiber arts.
