Michelle Venetucci is an economic anthropologist with a focus on Silicon Valley. Her work explores how the tech workforce’s middle class domestic expectation of stability helps reproduce the valley’s “high risk” speculative economic practices. She is currently a PhD candidate in the anthropology department at Yale University and a policy fellow at the Institution for Social and Policy Studies.
