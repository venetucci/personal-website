Michelle Venetucci is an anthropologist studying Silicon Valley. Her research interests include technology company workflows and workplace culture, and the social contexts of how new technologies are developed in Silicon Valley.

She's currently a PhD candidate in the Yale Anthropology department, with an emphasis in Science & Technology Studies (STS), and is conducting fieldwork in the San Francisco Bay Area between June 2022 and August 2023. She was previously a product designer at GitHub, Lyft, and thoughtbot. Sometimes she does fiber arts.
